The Homelessness Epidemic, A Struggle Worldwide

Homelessness is when someone doesn’t have a stable or permanent place to call home whether you are out living on the streets, staying in a shelter, sleeping in a car, or crashing temporarily with friends or family. There are many reasons why people experience homelessness such as losing a job, struggling with money, not being able to afford housing, dealing with mental health issues, or escaping unsafe situations like domestic violence. It’s something that can happen to anyone and has a big impact on people’s lives, making it hard to feel secure or move forward. Millions of people everyday are left without a place to truly call home. Learn more about the homelessness epidemic and what you can do about it below.
